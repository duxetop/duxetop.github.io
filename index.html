Here's how to use this:
1: For one word food items you can enter them into the input field as is. If the product being searched for is more than one word follow this example: Cheddar%20Cheese
Between two words you must add the %20 or else you wont get any results back.

2: The same rule stated above applies to multiple parameters, but, the %20 changes to %2C. You can choose parameters from the following list:
item_name, item_id, brand_name
